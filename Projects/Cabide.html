Details

This group project was intended to develop a piece of furniture suitable for the target audience and the identity of the Portuguese brand: Adico.

The main objectives of this project were to develop skills in the area of ​​representing ideas in product design (sketching), develop solid project concepts, capable of generating innovative and relevant products, select materials, finishes and production methods suitable for the desired purposes, execute rigorous technical drawings of the product and build product study and presentation models with relevant features.

Unfortunately, this project had a bad balance of workload between members, in the first phase, to create our idea, I was the main one keeping solid new and more ideas coming, to present and defend to the professor, after this most principal phase we worked better to start developing and making our idea stronger, as a team we came to investigate measurements and types of products our terrace hanger could have and other major ideas and details and from that point we divided the work, in my part, I started creating our report, make the 3D of our product and the rigorous technical drawings.
